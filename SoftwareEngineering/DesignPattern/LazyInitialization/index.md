Lazy Initialization pattern
===

<img src='./.img/FactoryMethod.jpg' style="background-color: white; padding: 10px;"/>

The tactic of delaying the creation of an object, the calculation of a value, or some other expensive process until the first time it is needed. It is a kind of lazy evaluation that refers specifically to the instantiation of objects or other resources.
